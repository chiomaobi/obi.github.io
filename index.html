Youth Volunteer

Why Did we Create Youth Volunteer

Youth Volunteer is a oraganization that provides service and support for many people who need help around the world. We believe that it is important for everyone to help out people in need. We love helping people amd seeing their lives change. We know that finding places to volunteer gets really hard sometimes, especially finding a place where you would enjoy your time. So that is why we created this website to make it the easiest way possible to find the physical and social benefits of volunteering. This organization allows young folks all around the world to get together and start volunteering at a young age.

How Does it Work

On our map tab there is a map, you'll input the city or zip you would like to volunteer in, a list will pop up of the nearest volunteer centers will be listed as well as their contact informaton. You can volunteer anywhere around the world.

Why You Should Volunteer

Aside from probably completing your mandatory school service hours, volunteering has many benefits such as meeting new people, feeling more involved in your community, building new connections, and learning something new each day. We encourage everyone to volunteer some point in their life because it can change someones life, even if it is not much.

Mission Statement

All around the world, there are many people who are suffering and need our help. As we said before, We truly believe that helping others is important in life and we hope that everyone knows this.

Be helpful in any way you can, Spread happiness to others, give to others, believe and hope always.

And remember, "The greatest good is what we do for one another."

We included a travel option because we want everyone to experience other cultures while they volunteer. This wonderful journey of changing lives will take place every summer when students are out of school. We will provide scholarships and stipeneds for the volunteers that want to travel to another country.

Why it's important to volunteer while traveling

We believe it is important, because it is a great opportunity to meet new people, experience new things and get to see the world while helping.

Sometimes in life, we forget about the little things around us and sometimes we don't stop to think about what's really important.

Their are many problems that have happened in this world and still today things are happening.

The great thing about traveling around the world is that you have the opportunity to save many lives.

There are people in Africa, Asia, and everywhere else that need our help. Lets let today be the day that we help someone, whether it's giving them food or a roof to live under, either one will be life changing for them.

The work programme itself was varied and enjoyable. Some physical work involving chopping down bushes, trees etc and planting, but mainly it was data monitoring of animals and their behaviour. In my time there I learned so much about each animal type and their behaviour.- Anonymous

You get to work with so many different people and really see the impact you are having in their lives, both through your chosen project and for the extra time spent reading with a child or playing sports. On top of the volunteering, you get to experience and learn about the Zambian culture as well as explore the country, for example, by going on safari or doing a bridge swing over Victoria Falls. - Anonymous

Peru is so beautiful. There are so many historical buildings and ruins, you could spend weeks there just exploring it all. Volunteering at the after school program was a lot of fun. The kids were so sweet and eager to learn. Also, my Spanish classes were very fun and my understanding of the language improved greatly. Machu Picchu is also a must and I recommend taking the train there because the scenery is breath taking. I enjoyed every single minute and would definitely return! -Anonymous
